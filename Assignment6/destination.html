Discover Paris

Paris, France

Paris is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. Known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture, it is a must-visit destination.

3-Day Paris Itinerary

Day 1: Eiffel Tower & Seine River Cruise

Start your tour by visiting the symbol of Paris, The Eiffel Tower. Relax and sail on the Seine River to view more of Paris from its waters.

Day 2: Louvre Museum & Champs-Élysées

Visit the world-famous Louvre and its amazing artworks. Before or after your visit, stroll along the beautiful Champs-Élysées, go shopping or sit in a café.

Day 3: Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur Basilica

Explore the bohemian atmosphere of Montmartre, a historic and artistic neighborhood. Be sure to end with a visit to the Sacre-Coeur for the most beautiful views of Paris.
